About Us

Toshi Engineering Works ‘State-of-the-art’ Tube Mills

Headed by qualified mechanical engineers having more than four decade’s expertise in the field of tube mill industries. Toshi supplies modern HFIW Tube mills with most advanced entry section, quick changing mill section, variety of fling cutoff machines and accurate tooling to meet the stringent requirements of general engineering, automotive, structural, boiler, heat exchanger and other relevant applications. Toshi also supplies various tube finishing equipment like End Facing, Threading, Straightening, Hydro testing etc. The Tube Mills robust in design, employ highly efficient helical drive system and various levels of automation to suit customer’s requirements. Special shaping stands and combination of Turks head ensure complex hollow section production with precise quality. Cutoff machine can be selected according to productivity and application like metal saw cutoff, cold saw cutoff, Disc cutoff. Tooling are made of special tool steel to achieve maximum wear resistance and greater life.

Toshi provides complete services start from projects engineering right up to successful commissioning, performance trails, startup assistance & after sales services, all under one roof.
